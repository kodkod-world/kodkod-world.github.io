Project S.O.P

A series of events revolving around the ‘Standard Operation Procedure’ (S.O.P.) installation which celebrates the S.O.P. rituals and commemorates the engagement while carrying out S.O.P. tasks, aiming to provoke conversations around human-tech relationships.

As the project focus, S.O.P. installation is an interactive sculpture that transforms mundane rituals into delightful performative experience. It will be presented as the S.O.P. station of KongsiKL during 1st~31st Nov 2021.

About

KodKod is a Kuala Lumpur based collective that specialises on crafting sensorial experiences and investigating design interactions with a discourse to tame 21st century digital technology so that it could cohabit with society harmoniously.

The collective currently comprises interaction designer, spatial designer, narrative composer and researcher who want to bring happiness to the society through the lens of media, art science and technology.
